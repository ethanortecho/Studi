Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password from the login screen by tapping "Forgot Password" and following the email instructions.
How is my Flow Score calculated?
Your Flow Score is based on focus ratings, study duration patterns, break hygiene, and deep work sessions. Scores range from 0-1000.
Can I sync my data across devices?
Yes! Your study data syncs automatically when you're logged into the same account on multiple devices.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your study data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.

Response Time

We typically respond to support requests within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your email subject line.
